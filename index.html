Fantasy Lottery Tool

A sharp, reliable tool for running fair and exciting draft lotteries. Built from the ground up with fantasy basketball at its core, but ready for any format — NBA, NFL, EPL, MLB, NHL or your custom league. Whether it's a weighted dynasty draw or a clean-slate redraft, this app gives you everything you need to run your lottery with clarity and confidence.

Key Features

  • Deep Lottery Support: Run lotteries for up to 30 teams with custom names
  • Weighted Odds: Using lottery ball system — ideal for dynasty formats
  • Redraft Support: Assign equal weight for a fully random order
  • Live Pick Reveal: Animated draft results
  • Auto-Draft Capabilities: Draft your picks at your pace, your choice
  • Draftboard Export: clean, polished, and ready to share with your league
